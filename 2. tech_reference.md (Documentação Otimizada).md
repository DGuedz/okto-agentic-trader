\# OKTO TECH STACK REFERENCE

\#\# 1\. CCXT (Unified CEX API)  
\- \*\*Init:\*\* \`exchange \= ccxt.binance({'apiKey': key, 'secret': sec})\`  
\- \*\*Balance:\*\* \`exchange.fetch\_balance()\`  
\- \*\*Market Price:\*\* \`exchange.fetch\_ticker(symbol)\['last'\]\`  
\- \*\*Orders:\*\* \`exchange.create\_market\_buy\_order(symbol, amount)\`

\#\# 2\. FOUNDRY / CAST (Web3 Direct)  
\- \*\*Read:\*\* \`cast call \[CONTRACT\] "\[FUNC\]"\`  
\- \*\*Write:\*\* \`cast send \[CONTRACT\] "\[FUNC\]" \--private-key $PK \--rpc-url $RPC\`  
\- \*\*Balance:\*\* \`cast balance \[ADDR\] \--rpc-url $RPC\`

\#\# 3\. SDD & VSC WORKFLOW  
\- \*\*Spec First:\*\* Read \`.yaml\` config before any \`.py\` execution.  
\- \*\*Trace IDE:\*\* Log all execution steps in \`logs/trace.log\`.  
\- \*\*Token Saver:\*\* Prefer YAML/MD over JSON. Never rewrite full code; provide patches.
