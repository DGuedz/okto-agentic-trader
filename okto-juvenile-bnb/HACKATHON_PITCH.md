3.  **Context Switching:** Managing liquidity requires leaving the IDE to visit slow, complex dApp UIs.

## üêô THE SOLUTION: OKTO (On-Demand Liquidity)
Okto is a **Headless Liquidity Protocol** that integrates directly into your development workflow. It transforms your terminal into a **Revenue-Generating Node**.

### üõ†Ô∏è How It Works (The "Code-to-Yield" Pipeline)
1.  **You Code:** Okto runs silently in the background of your terminal.
2.  **Okto Farms:** It detects idle compute/network resources and executes **On-Demand Liquidity (ODL)** strategies.
    *   **Scalping:** Micro-trades on Binance Futures (via CCXT) to generate alpha.
    *   **Bridging:** Profits are automatically bridged to **BNB Chain**.
    *   **Farming:** Capital is deployed into **Aster DEX** (Perpetual Vaults) to earn real yield.
3.  **You Earn:** Yield is reported directly in your terminal logs (`[OKTO] YIELD: +$4.20 | GAS: 0.05 Gwei`).

## üß† CORE TECHNOLOGY: HEADLESS FINANCE SELF BANK
We stripped away the UI to focus on **pure execution efficiency**.

### 1. The "Confluence Supreme" Engine (Active)
*   **Vector Analysis:** RSI + Bollinger + OBI (Order Book Imbalance).
*   **Dark Pool Execution:** Splits orders to minimize slippage and hide intent.
*   **Safety Rails:** `specs/genesis.yaml` defines strict risk limits (Max Gas, Max Drawdown).

### 2. The Aster Tentacle (Passive)
*   **Auto-Compound:**Harvests yields from Aster DEX and reinvests.
*   **Risk-Adjusted Rebalancing:** Moves capital between "Hunting" (Binance) and "Farming" (Aster) based on volatility.
